****Groove Bot V2.0.0****
Hello! I am GrooveBot, a bot specially coded for the r/Animusic Discord server!
If you have any issues/requests contact Denyul or sunsetdev.
>>> ~~---------------~~:tools: Commands :tools:~~----------------~~
**fact** - Retrieves an Animusic fact.
**getalbums** - Retrieves all albums.
**getalbum** `[acronym]` - Retrieves all music within an album.
**getmusic** `[acronym]` - Retrieves url of music and can be played in a voice channel.
**getabbreviations** - Retrieves all Animusic specific abbreviations.
**neuropol** `[message]` - Parses all text into neuropol.
**stop** - Stops audio transmission.
**leave** - Removes bot from voice channel.
